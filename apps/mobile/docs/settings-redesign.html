FAQ

Quick answers to the questions dads ask us most.
9:415G ▮ 100%
SettingsFAQ
Does one subscription cover both parents?
Yes — one subscription per family. Invite your partner and you both get full access.
Can I keep using it after the baby's born?
Yes. Content continues through the newborn months and into toddlerhood (18+ months).
How do the weekly briefings work?
Each week you get a short, dad-focused briefing on the baby, your partner, and what to do.
How do I invite my partner?
Go to Family, share your invite code, and they enter it at signup.
Is my family's data private?
Yes. Your logs and tasks are private to your family and never sold.
How do I cancel?
Manage your plan in Subscription; cancel anytime, no questions asked.
