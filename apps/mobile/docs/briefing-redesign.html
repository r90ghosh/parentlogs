Briefing screen — 3 redesign directions

Same content and brand, four ways to fix the density. All of them strip the heavy stuff from the live app — no floating particles, no 3D tilt, no blur-stacking, no animate-everything. Each also kills the 104-week pill bar, stops dumping every section open at once, and gives the page a real type hierarchy with breathing room. Direction 4 (“Field Manual”) goes furthest on your “clean & elegant for dads” note — no emoji, no colored boxes, one accent, zero motion. Sample week: Week 24. Tap accordions, chapter pills, and steppers to try them.

👶Baby Update

Baby crosses the “viability” line this week — around 24 weeks, survival odds outside the womb jump sharply as the lungs develop. Baby is now about 12 inches and 1.3 pounds, with a fully formed face and taste buds coming online. The inner ear is finished, so baby can sense which way is up — and is starting to respond to your voice.

💝What She's Experiencing

Her bump is unmistakable now and her center of gravity is shifting — expect backaches and a clumsiness she won't mention. Braxton Hicks (practice contractions) may begin: tightening that comes and goes. She'll likely book the glucose screening soon. Heartburn and swollen ankles are common and normal.

🎯Your Focus This Week
Four things worth doing this week:
  • Start the nursery conversation — furniture lead times run 8–12 weeks.
  • Go to the glucose screening if you can. It's a long one (1–2 hrs).
  • Talk to baby out loud — hearing works now and it builds the bond early.
  • Confirm the hospital and pediatrician are in-network.
