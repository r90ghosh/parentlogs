The Voice

Your baby is learning the music of language — and your voice is the instrument. Talk, narrate, and read out loud; every word is building their brain.

Week 10 · Coos & first vowels
In this briefing
Baby update
Cooing and babbling — open vowels like “oooh” and “ahhh.” These are the building blocks of language. When you respond, you’re teaching conversation rhythm.
What she’s experiencing
Hair loss (postpartum shed). Tell her she’s beautiful — it goes further than you think.
Your focus this week
  • Read simple board books daily — your voice is building their brain
  • Narrate your life to the baby — it builds language recognition
  • Tummy time 5+ min per day — essential for rolling and sitting
From the trenches
Your baby is starting to recognize your voice and respond with coos and babbles. Talk to them constantly. Narrate what you’re doing: “Dad is making coffee, now Dad is pouring milk.” It feels ridiculous. It builds their language foundation.
Relationship check-in
Tell her she’s beautiful. Postpartum hair loss is starting and it’s devastating for many women. A genuine compliment goes further than you think.
Coming up
Grabbing. Around now, baby starts reaching for and grabbing objects.
