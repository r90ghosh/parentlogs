⏱ 36-hour waitGoogle takes up to 36 hours to validate the service account. Start this early. RevenueCat will error until validation completes.
1.4Create Android-specific subscription & one-time products
⚠ Lifetime ≠ subscription on AndroidGoogle Play doesn't support "lifetime subscriptions." Lifetime must be a managed in-app product, not a subscription product. RevenueCat handles this via its Offerings/Packages layer — iOS lifetime SKU and Android managed product share the same RC package.
