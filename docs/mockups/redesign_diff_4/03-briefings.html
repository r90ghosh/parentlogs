Your Baby at Week 24

About the size of a cantaloupe  ·  ~600 grams

24 weeks

Your baby's hearing is now fully developed. They can hear your voice, music, and even the rhythm of your heartbeat. This is a beautiful time to start talking and singing to your little one.

The facial features are becoming more defined, and fat is beginning to accumulate beneath the translucent skin, giving your baby a more rounded appearance with every passing day.

Her Experience This Week

  • Glucose tolerance test
    Typically scheduled around this time — a routine screen for gestational diabetes.
  • Back pain increasing
    The growing belly shifts her center of gravity, putting pressure on lower back muscles.
  • Nesting instincts starting
    A strong urge to prepare the home and organize for the baby's arrival may begin now.
  • Energy fluctuations
    Good days and exhausting days side by side — rest when the body asks for it.
