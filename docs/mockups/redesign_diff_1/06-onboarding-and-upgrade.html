Frequently asked

Does my partner also get premium access?
Yes — one subscription covers your entire family. When you subscribe, your partner automatically gets full premium access too. The Dad Center is built for both parents, not just one.
Can I cancel anytime?
Monthly and yearly plans can be cancelled anytime from your account settings. You'll keep premium access until the end of your billing period. The lifetime plan is a one-time purchase with no recurring charges.
What can I do on the free plan?
The free plan includes all tasks in your 30-day rolling window, full briefing access for your first 4 weeks, push notifications for 30 days, and 4 starter checklists. It's genuinely useful — not a crippled trial. Upgrade when you want more.
