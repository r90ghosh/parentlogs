Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Weekly Briefings

Personalized weekly summaries of what matters most — what's happening with baby, what your partner needs, and what you should be doing right now.

Smart Tasks

47 stage-appropriate tasks across the full journey. 30-day rolling window keeps it manageable. No overwhelm, just clear next actions.

Mood Check-ins

Daily emotional tracking designed for dads. Flag what's weighing on you. Build self-awareness. The app surfaces patterns so you can act on them.

Dad Journey

Seven pillars of modern fatherhood: knowledge, planning, finances, anxiety, baby bonding, your relationship, and extended family. Challenges and growth built in.

Partner Sync

One subscription per family. Both partners share access. Role-aware content: moms and dads see what's relevant to them. The app plays neutral third party.

Budget Planner

Built-in financial planning for the cost of a new baby. Track spending, set targets, and go into parenthood without the financial blindspot most new dads hit.
